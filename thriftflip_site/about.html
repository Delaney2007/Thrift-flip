Our Story

ThriftFlip began as a simple idea: extend the life of garments and make fashion with less waste. We source thrifted items and rework them into fresh, wearable pieces. Each transformation is documented so you can see the impact of upcycling in action.

Why Upcycling?

Upcycling reduces landfill waste and celebrates creativity. By choosing one-of-a-kind pieces, our customers reduce demand for new production and support sustainable practices.

Our Promise

We use low-impact dyes and repair techniques. We minimise data collection and are transparent about how we use the information you provide.
